Aerial Arts

Getting Started!

Some people may think that aerial arts are daunting and difficult to begin doing. While it may seem that way, the only way to get better is by practicing. The strength comes with continued training! Some of the notable aerial schools in Portland, OR are Night Flight Aerial, A-WOL Dance, and The Circus Project.

Apparatuses to Choose From

Circus arts have a wide variety of disciplines, and aerial is no different. You can choose to train on whatever apparatus suits your own aesthetic or comfort, to a certain extent.

    You can choose from:
  • Silks/Fabric/Tissu
  • Static Trapeze
  • Dance Trapeze
  • Lyra/Hoop/Circeau
    Class Structure:
  1. Warm up on floor
  2. Warm up on apparatus
  3. Techique refinement
  4. Learn new skills
  5. Review old material
  6. Cool down on floor

Safety

While some aspects of aerial may seem fun and easy, and they might look super cool, safety is always the top priority.
